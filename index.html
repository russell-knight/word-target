Word Target

Welcome to Word Target. To get started please choose from one of the following game styles. Regular game has no time limit and allows the player to play the game at their own pace. Blitz game is a timed version that lets the player test their wits against the clock.

The aim of the game is to find words of four letters or more. Every word must include the centre letter and each letter may only be used once. There is at least one 9-letter word to be found.
